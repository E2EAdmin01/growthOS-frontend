Frequently asked questions

Do I need any SEO knowledge to use RankActions?
No. RankActions is built specifically for people who aren't SEO experts. Every recommendation is written in plain English with a clear explanation of why it matters and exactly what to do.
How does it connect to my website?
RankActions connects to Google Search Console with one click — no code, no plugins, no installation. You grant read-only access, which means we can see your data but can never make changes to your site.
Is my data safe?
Yes. We only request read-only access to Search Console. We don't store your website's content — only your access token, which is encrypted. You can disconnect at any time.
How is this different from Google Search Console itself?
Search Console shows you the data. RankActions tells you what to do with it. We take your raw rankings and traffic numbers, identify the highest-impact opportunities, and give you specific, actionable fixes written by AI.
Can I use it for client websites?
Individual and Business each cover one website. The Agency plan supports multiple websites, each with its own action list, content strategy, link building pipeline and reports — all managed from one dashboard. Contact us to tailor an Agency plan to the number of sites you manage.
What is the content strategy planner?
The strategy planner analyses your Search Console keyword data and creates a pillar content plan — one comprehensive authority page plus 6-8 supporting blog posts. Each post targets a specific long-tail keyword and links back to the pillar, building topical authority with Google. Click "Write" on any post to generate it instantly with our AI content generator.
How does the link building feature work?
Our AI analyses your website and industry, then generates 8 specific link building opportunities — a mix of easy wins (directories, testimonials), medium effort (guest posts, resource pages) and harder targets (press coverage, partnerships).

Powered by Google's Gemini AI grounded in live Google Search, so opportunities reference real, current sites — look for the "✓ Verified by Google" badge on each one.

Each opportunity includes the target site, step-by-step instructions, and an AI-written outreach email you can personalise and send. Track your prospects through a pipeline from identified to secured.
Do I get weekly email reports?
Yes — every Monday morning you receive a branded email showing your traffic, rankings, and click-through rate compared to the previous week. It includes your top 3 actions for the week, any keywords close to reaching page 1, and an AI-written performance summary. You don't even need to log in.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel any time — no contracts, no cancellation fees. You keep access until the end of your billing period and can export your data at any time.
